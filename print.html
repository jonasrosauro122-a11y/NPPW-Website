NPPW Operations Automation Executive Summary

This print-ready page summarizes the proposed VA + automation support model for NPPW. It is designed for leadership review and PDF export.

Core objective

Reduce repetitive administrative load, improve ServiceChannel verification quality, and create cleaner operational reporting.

Primary systems

Acuity, Zapier, Jobber, ServiceChannel, and Google Sheets / internal tools.

Primary staffing model

Dedicated VA support for intake, work order administration, verification, and documentation.

Proposed flow

  1. Booking or request enters through Acuity or office intake.
  2. VA validates required fields and prevents bad data from moving downstream.
  3. Zapier transfers the clean record into Jobber.
  4. Operations team uses Jobber for scheduling and invoicing preparation.
  5. After field work, the VA verifies proof inside ServiceChannel.
  6. Status, issues, and trends are logged in Google Sheets for management visibility.

Top training priorities

  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • ServiceChannel work order management
  • Vendor verification & compliance
  • System ecosystem awareness
  • Reporting & documentation
  • Future marketing / branding support

Suggested org hierarchy

  • CEO / Leadership
  • Operations Manager / Specialist Lead
  • Automation / Process Owner
  • VA – Intake & Data Support
  • VA – Verification & Compliance
  • VA – Marketing / Branding Support

Business case summary

Based on discovery, focused verification alone can consume around four hours per day for one specialist’s queue. A trained VA supported by simple automation can help absorb repetitive checks, improve ticket accuracy, reduce manual re-entry, and create cleaner leadership reporting.

Implementation recommendation

  1. Start with work order verification, ticket management, and Acuity → Jobber support.
  2. Train the VA using the 15-day onboarding program.
  3. Pilot with a defined specialist or location group.
  4. Refine automation and reporting.
  5. Expand into marketing, branding, and SEO admin support.
