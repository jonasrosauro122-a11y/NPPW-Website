Core training modules

All modules are still included, with improved visualization and stronger explanation.

1. Attention to Detail & Accuracy Training

Critical foundation that teaches the VA how to spot errors before they affect operations.

  • Data accuracy drills and error spotting
  • Before/after validation exercises
  • Double-check protocol system
  • Real work order review simulations
2. ServiceChannel Work Order Management Training

Explains the work order lifecycle and how the VA keeps tickets accurate.

  • Create → dispatch → complete → close workflow
  • Vendor assignment logic and position 1 vendor awareness
  • Ticket updates and documentation discipline
  • Photo and timestamp verification rules
  • Handling missing check-ins or incomplete jobs
3. Data Transfer & Multi-System Operations Training

Teaches clean data movement across tools to avoid rework and duplicates.

  • Cross-system data mapping
  • Preventing duplicate entries
  • SOP-based data transfer flow
  • System role clarity
4. Vendor Verification & Compliance

High-priority module focused on proof validation, red flags, and escalation judgment.

  • Valid vs invalid job completion
  • Time-on-site validation rules
  • Photo compliance checks
  • Red flag identification
  • Escalation workflow for non-compliance
5. Communication & Phone Operations Training

Prepares the VA to handle incoming calls and document outcomes professionally.

  • Professional call handling scripts
  • Ticket update communication format
  • Vendor communication standards
  • Escalation language and protocols
  • Logging phone updates into systems
6. Checklist & SOP Discipline Training

Ensures no-step-skipping behavior and strong process control.

  • Step-by-step checklist execution
  • Task completion verification
  • No-step-skipping discipline system
  • Standard operating workflow practice
7. System Ecosystem Awareness Training

Builds understanding of how ServiceChannel, Jobber, Acuity, and internal tools connect.

  • System purpose mapping
  • Data flow between platforms
  • Risk awareness such as Acuity double-booking limitations
  • System boundaries and usage rules
8. Reporting & Documentation Training

Provides visibility for leadership and supports issue tracking.

  • Daily and weekly reporting structure
  • Issue tracking logs
  • Performance summary creation
  • Escalation documentation standards
9. Marketing & Branding Support Training

Introduces simple growth support work for future expansion.

  • Basic SEO principles
  • Google Business Profile updates
  • Service description consistency
  • Simple content updates
  • Brand tone alignment

How the VA learns the exact review path.

4

Check count, quality, and relevance of proof images.

Photo compliance
5

Decision / escalate

Mark verified, needs clarification, or escalate non-compliance.

6

Document result

Log into reporting tracker for specialist and leadership visibility.

Reporting

Red flags trainees must learn

  • No check-in or no check-out
  • Suspiciously short time on site
  • Missing, duplicated, or unclear photos
  • Notes that do not explain the service outcome
  • Any mismatch between proof and expected work

What “good review” looks like

  • Follows checklist without skipping steps
  • Uses standards consistently across tickets
  • Documents why a ticket passed or failed
  • Escalates quickly with the right language
  • Updates logs so leadership can see trends

Visual module focus • System Ecosystem Awareness

How the VA understands multiple platforms as one operating system.

System 1

Acuity

Booking intake source. Good for capturing requests, but it needs a validation gate to prevent errors and overbooking issues.

System 2

Zapier

Automation connector. Moves validated information between systems through mapped fields and rules.

System 3

Jobber

Operational scheduling and invoicing-prep environment. This is where downstream operations gain visibility.

System 4

ServiceChannel + Sheets

ServiceChannel manages proof review, while Sheets keeps reporting visible for leadership and issue tracking.

Awareness outcome

Know each system’s purpose

The trainee learns where work starts, where it gets reviewed, and where it gets reported.

Avoid duplicate work

The trainee learns what should not be re-entered manually without reason.

Protect handoff quality

The trainee understands how errors in one tool affect the next tool.

Support leadership visibility

The trainee knows how reporting turns system activity into management insight.

15-day training roadmap

Simple but deep enough for leadership review and internal execution.

Week 1 • Foundation

Context, systems, accuracy, and SOP discipline.

Day 1NPPW business model, services, recurring work order environment, and VA role overview.
Day 2Attention to detail drills and double-check discipline.
Day 3System ecosystem overview: Acuity, Zapier, Jobber, ServiceChannel, Google Sheets.
Day 4Work order lifecycle and documentation standards.
Day 5Checklist and SOP workshop with graded exercises.

Week 2 • Operations & compliance

Practical queue review and exception handling.

Day 6ServiceChannel work order management and ticket updates.
Day 7Photo / timestamp verification and proof logic.
Day 8Vendor verification and compliance review drills.
Day 9Escalation workflow for invalid or incomplete proof.
Day 10Phone communication and documentation training.

Week 3 • Data flow & readiness

Automation flow, reporting, simulation, and sign-off.

Day 11Cross-system data mapping and Acuity → Jobber flow practice.
Day 12Google Sheets reporting, issue logs, and leadership summaries.
Day 13End-to-end scenario simulation with pass/fail review.
Day 14Shadowing or supervised live-practice queue.
Day 15Final assessment, QA calibration, and readiness sign-off.

Assessment framework

How NPPW can evaluate training success.

Accuracy score

Measure how often the trainee catches errors and completes the checklist correctly.

Verification quality

Review whether the trainee classifies tickets correctly and uses escalation appropriately.

Data transfer quality

Check if the trainee correctly handles field mapping and exception management.

Reporting quality

Evaluate note clarity, issue logs, and management summary usefulness.
